Press the goal schemas above to get tips on how to achieve that goal!

For a complex setup, see below.

When you first spawn on your island, you'll be on a plain island with grass, stone clusters, and a tall oak tree, without any chests and with nothing.

Chop the tree's logs and leaves. Plant the saplings around the blocks spread around the island, then craft all the logs into planks.

Craft a crafting table and then make a wooden pickax. Mine the stone that's above the grass, craft a stone pickax, and then mine the grass that's in a cluster above the rest of the ground.

Use the dirt you've collected to bridge to the stone island in the distance. Mine the clay above the stone, then go down into the tunnel behind Jerry and loot the chest down there. Come back up, talk to Jerry, and then hop through the portal.

It might sound boring and unnecessary, but after you get to the hub, you should talk to all the villagers to get information about the game and coins! The locations are:

  • Leo: Standing in front of the portal you came out of. This is in plain sight.

  • Jack: Loop around the portal you came out of.

  • Duke: Past Leo, standing in front of the large map.

  • Stella: Go to the right of the Salesman out to the farm, standing to the left about halfway through the entrance to the farm.

  • Vex: Go up and to the left diagonally to exactly reach.

  • Jamie: Go behind the portal and over to the forest, to the left chunk just before reaching it.

  • Tom: To the right of Jack, behind leaves next to the potato farm.

  • Felix: After going down the stairs to the mine, stop and turn left.

  • Lynn: After finding Felix, find the blacksmith to the left of the mine, then look slightly to the right.

  • Ryu: Pass Stella, then look just in front of the farm.

  • Andrew: Up and to the right diagonally from Tom, exactly.

  • Liam: Find the librarian near the mine, then sprint behind it and beyond the tree until a small area appears with Liam. This is the hardest villager to find.
